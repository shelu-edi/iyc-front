Bare Face Campaign 2023
Every woman is endowed with a one-of-a-kind natural beauty. The International Youth Council (IYC) believes that International Women's Day should be a day that promotes self-love and optimism. The IYC together with the Miss England bare-face winner 22 Melisa Raouf launched a bare-face campaign on International Women’s Day to emphasize the point that a woman does not have to feel insecure if she keeps her face bare and to accept and cherish her natural beauty without the pressure of filters or the standards of artificial beauty. Several women were strongly moved and joined this movement.

Vision
A world in which all nations provide a platform for inclusive and sustainable youth development
Mission
The IYC (International Youth Council) seeks to create an environment in which the youth’s opinions are heard and appreciated by assisting nations and youth-led groups with policy development, leadership skills, and resilience building to support inclusive and sustainable youth development that will contribute to the achievement of the UN SDGs (Sustainable Development Goals).

Trusted Partnerships
Become an active partner of the International Youth Council and take part in the movement to enhance the lives of youth from disadvantaged backgrounds. We are committed to mobilizing resources to execute the 2030 Agenda through a revived Global Partnership for Sustainable Development, with special emphasis on the poorest and most vulnerable people. Any organization that shares our vision and values is invited to collaborate with us.

Projects
The Green Conference
In 2013, the IYC chapter of Sri Lanka organized "The Green Conference" under the direction of the IYC chairperson Sadham D Zarjahan.
International Conference on Youth, Peace, and Violent Extremism
UN Security Council Resolution 2250 "Plan of Action to Prevent Violent Extremism," was advanced by the hosting of the International Conference on Youth, Peace, and Violent Extremism at Hatten Place in Melaka, Malaysia from July 30th to August 3rd, 2018.
International Capacity Building Workshop on Green Growth
The International Capacity Building Workshop on Green Growth was held in the Ramada Plaza in Melaka, Malaysia, from July 19 to July 23, 2017.
