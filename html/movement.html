Get involved as a Private Sector Supporter
As a private sector supporter, you will have the opportunity to make a significant difference in the lives of those in need. Your support will enable us to continue our vital work and expand our programs to reach more people. You will be part of a community of like-minded individuals and organizations who are passionate about making a positive impact on the world.

There are many ways you can support IYC as a private-sector supporter. You can make a financial contribution, sponsor a program or initiative, or donate goods or services. You can also get involved by volunteering your time and expertise, joining our events and campaigns, or advocating for our cause in your community.

In return, we offer our private sector supporters the satisfaction of knowing that their support is making a real difference in the world. We will keep you updated on the progress of our programs and initiatives, and we will work closely with you to ensure that your support is used effectively and efficiently.

We are open to working with any organisation that shares the same values and vision. Please write the following IYC Executive Committee Member or book an appointment here to discuss: https://fb.com/book/IYCconnect

Organize an event
Would you like to support IYC’s special events or help spread the word about our programs for young people? There are many ways that you can contribute and participate. Here are some ideas:
  1. Host an International Youth Day with IYC in your local community.
  2. Organize a discussion at your schools, Universities, place of work, or community centre on Inclusive Youth Development. Contact your nearest IYC office and ask for ideas, and maybe even a speaker.
  3. Hold a debate on a topic important to you – for example, Role is a Young Leader? What is Inclusive Youth Development? What can youth and communities do to help?
