WORLDWIDE
The International Youth Council has been able to effectively distribute its services to UN member countries in multiple continents all over the world through the initiation of IYC chapters around the globe.

Initially, the IYC was only to be held in the United States. Nonetheless, it has grown into a leading youth organization with over 22,000 members in 72 United Nations member countries, including universities, private organizations, and youth councils. Members of the organization have carried out a wide range of projects on the ground, from gender equality to sustainable development. They have actively participated in significant international decision-making processes as a force within the system of United Nations' mandates of MDGs and SDGs since 2015s

By setting up IYC chapters all around the world, the International Youth Council had been able to successfully communicate with and disseminate its services to UN member countries across several continents

Initially, the International Youth Council planned to establish its headquarters only in the United States. Despite this, it evolved into a large youth organization with over 22,000 members in 72 United Nations member countries, including colleges, business groups, and youth councils. Members of the organization have actively participated in key international decision-making processes as a force within the framework of the MDGs and SDGs mandates of the United Nations since 2015. On the ground, members of the organization have worked on many issues, from gender equality to sustainable development. The IYC team formally revoked all IYC memberships and closed all IYC chapters on December 31st, 2022. The IYC's new country program, international volunteer program, and global projects were officially launched on January 1st, 2023.
